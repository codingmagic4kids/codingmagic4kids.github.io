CodingMagic4Kids

Fun and Interesting Programming Courses for Kids

The idea behind 'Coding Magic' is to introduce programming concepts to kids and teens by making them 'do' cool projects. Learning, be it Programming or anything for that matter, happens only when the student is interested and motivated. 'Coding Magic', empowers the students to code, by incrementally building their coding-confidence with fun hands-on projects, right from session-1.
